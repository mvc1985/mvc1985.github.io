We support local businesses in Toronto

If you can, please check out and support these local businesses

Spacing

Based on Spacing Magazine, which looks at the many urban issues of Canada’s largest city, the Spacing Store has both a brick-and-mortar store at 401 Richmond Street in downtown Toronto and an on-line store at www.spacing.ca. Spacing works directly with local designers, artists, and makers to offer a selection of merchandise like no other shop in Toronto.

We love this shop and the fine people who run it! During April and May, 2020, you can get a 15% discount if you type TWINTOWN in the discount code at check out.

The People’s Pint Brewing Company

This is a great brewpub in the Junction Triangle with an array of craft beers on tap. They currently have pick-up and delivery options.

Visit them at 90 Cawthra Ave Unit #101 and on-line at www.peoplespint.com

Mana Pool

This family-run board game bistro in the High Park area of Toronto was one of the first venues in Toronto that held regular TwinTownTrivia quizzes.

Enjoy and purchase a wide range of board games, card games and accessories via their website www.manapool.ca
